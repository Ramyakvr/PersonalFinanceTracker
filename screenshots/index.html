FinBoom — Wireframes

Hand-built HTML wireframes derived from the research walk-through. Open each file in a browser.

Why HTML, not PNG? The browser automation tool's screenshots are sandboxed inside the Chrome extension and can't be persisted to this workspace. These hand-built wireframes encode layout, field names, and interaction affordances — which is what a rebuild needs anyway. Companion docs: FEATURES.md · SPEC.md.
